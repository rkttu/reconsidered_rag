## Why Apache 2.0?

Apache 2.0 provides balanced protection while being business-friendly:

- **Permissive License**: Allows commercial use, modification, and redistribution
- **Patent Protection**: Includes patent licensing terms
- **Compatibility**: Compatible with GPL and other open source licenses
- **No Copyleft**: Modifications can be kept proprietary
